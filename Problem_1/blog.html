My Appreciation For the Ottomans

I am deeply appreciative of Mohammad Al-Fatih, the Conqueror of Constantinople. His remarkable leadership and strategic acumen in capturing Constantinople in 1453 transformed history, marking the end of the Byzantine Empire and heralding the rise of the Ottoman Empire. His legacy of triumph and cultural exchange remains profoundly impactful.

My Trip To Turkey

Visiting Mohammad Al-Fatih Mosque and his grave in Turkey was awe-inspiring. Reflecting on his conquest of Constantinople, I felt profound appreciation for his legacy. My journey from America was long, but standing at his revered sites, I felt a deep connection to history and the enduring impact of his achievements.
